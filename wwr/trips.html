Los dientes del daiblo

An exciting ride through strong and turbulent waters, for those looking for a dose of adrenaline. The course of the trip is through the Peruvian river “Diente del diablo”, known for its strong currents and vertiginous speed along with waterfalls all along the river.

Miau Waters

Calm waters, beautiful scenery and many animals near you, this is the main characteristic of Miau Waters, a slow and calm river to take pictures of wildlife, is one of the most visited by professional photographers and family groups who want to come with their pets, because yes, in this trip you can come with your pet!

Oops, someone fell to the water

This ride takes you down a river with no sharp rocks or danger, perfect for children and teenagers who want a thrilling experience without risking danger and injury. But even so, the flow and the constant descent will make more than one of you fly out of the boat!
