SPLASH White Water Rafting

We are SPLASH, we are a company focused on giving the best river trips for all kinds of tastes and conditions, always supervised by our rescuers and professionals in the area.

History

It all started as a little hobby that our founder shared with friends, until she met interested people and she offered to guide them through “Meow Waters”, before that route was called that way, a long time ago believe it or not.

Thanks to that, little by little she built up a reputation as a knowledgeable, expert and river rescuer, everything went smoothly and that's how SPLASH was born. Now we have more active staff and many more routes available for all tastes in safe environments.
